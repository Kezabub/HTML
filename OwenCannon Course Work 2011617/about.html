About Us

Our commissioners are a social partnership made up of CEOs from large and small businesses, employment advisers and trade union representatives from across the UK. Our team of around 100 staff is based in London and South Yorkshire. UKCES is registered in England and Wales under company number 06425800.

What we Do?

We are a publicly funded, industry-led organisation that offers guidance on skills and employment issues in the UK.
